FAQ

What happens after my Merit access expires?

Yes. The code is open source (MIT). Self-hosting gives you the engine without the community substrate. The 38K+ waveform substrate is what the paid/merit tiers unlock.

Only if you explicitly register it as a pattern. Scoring and cascade analysis process code in memory and discard it. See our Privacy Policy.

What's the Merit threshold?

Why 7 days for the free tier?

Long enough to create real value for Premium/Merit users. Short enough that nobody is truly excluded. The Covenant principle: abundance for all.

Can I cancel Premium anytime?

Yes. Access continues through the end of your billing period. You drop to Free tier (7-day delay) afterward.
